The “Self-Confidence & Best-Self” Challenge:

Your organisation expects - and needs - employees who are Self-Confident and bring their Best-Self to the table, all the time, on all levels, as specialists, managers and leaders. They are expected to up-skill continuously, unlearn old habits and skills, then re-learn new ones, so that they can perform with unmatching self-confidence.

As they walk their career and life paths upward, your organisation will expect them to remain technically proficient in their fields, understand ever changing computer and software systems, be emotionally intelligent, spiritually mature, psychologically stable, outstanding communicators, presenters of note, values driven and much more - all this reflecting their levels of Self-Confidence and Best-Self. Welcome to the new reality in our VUCA - volatile, uncertain, complex, ambiguous - world in overdrive and it is only going to intensify.

Hence, many of your employees continuously develop themselves in order to become and remain self-confident, through opportunities provided by your organisation - where possible - or preferably also on their own. Because of the amazing information age we live in, many employees are able to read articles and books, listen to podcasts and watch videos, learn from others’ examples, receive mentoring, do courses, diplomas, more degrees, attend webcasts and engage other activities to sharpen the saw.

Important Questions to Consider

How do they:

1. Demonstrate that they have engaged in all their continuous development activities that are often readily available - almost prove that they have acquired the necessary skills and knowledge for that next responsibility?

2. How do they demonstrate that they have extracted relevant learnings, skills and knowledge, especially practical application, from all their development activities?

3. Even with formal certificates, diplomas and degrees that promise specific outcomes, how do they show that they have captured, internalized and practiced the relevant learnings from it? We all know that much of the content of these programs arent practically relevant. How do they show that they have prioritized the essentials, because they know what is and will be expected of them in that next role?

4. How do they navigate the transition from specialist to manager and formal leader, then adapt their continuous development activities accordingly?

5. How do you - in todays cost sensitive times - afford financially to continuously develop, upskill your employees real-time (all the time, continuously), so that they can match the real-time world?

It takes a village!

Solution = Me-Vision Academy

Me-Vision Academy is a dynamic virtual platform that represents much more than an ordinary academy. It will help you do all of the above and more, real-time, because we live in a real-time world:

1. We help individuals build what we refer to as Best-Self Confidence - not just self- confidence - from the beginning of their career as specialists all the way to junior and senior leadership positions. Authentic confidence that stems from a healthy True Self, a clear path towards the Best-Self, resulting in a healthy Self Image = Best-Self Confidence.

2. There are six Best-Self Confidence Levels inside the platform. Every year, as they receive 3000 + Continuous Best-Self (CBS) Points they move up a level - growing in the right kind of self-confidence. Its reachable, but enough of a stretch to ensure continuous development and not a scramble at the end of the year, just to tick the box - as we often see with the CPD process.

3. Every year they are rewarded with Me-Vision Academy certificates, declaring them “Best Self Confident” - professional, competent, ready, in their chosen skillset and on their current positional level.

4. We give your employees access to thousands of relevant internationally recognized certificates and diplomas, free. They - or your organisation - just need to purchase the actual digital or tangible certificate or diploma upon completion, at a reasonable price.

5. Inside their Personal Development Plans, employees electronically capture any development activities inside their Portfolio of Evidence (POE) - they are rewarded with Continuous Best-Self (CBS) Points for doing it, real-time (immediately).

6. They consciously capture their relevant learnings inside their POE - they are rewarded with CBS Points, real-time.

7. Based on their learnings they record goals, actions and commitments for proof of practical implementation - more real-time CBS Points.

8. They achieve their goals, actions and commitments - succeed at the practical implementation - more real-time CBS Points.

9. As they become their Best Self more and more, they can LEAD on forums with their knowledge, skills and expertise - more real-time CBS Points for practically leading.

10. They are now ready to be showcased on a Best-Self Confident Leaderboard, arranged by skillset (sector), position and country. You have line of sight over all employees, per skillset and positional level. The Leaderboard ensures full accountability by every participant to continuously develop themselves towards their Best-Self.

11. Not only your accountants and engineers - who often belong to outside organisations that hold them accountable to professional standards - but all your employees are held to the same standards - admin, sales, logistics, legal and so on. All of them need to earn 3000 CBS Points.

12. They will participate in activities that are a more surgical approach to their trustable, accountable Best-Self Confidence, resulting in emotional and spiritual fitness - more CBS Points and Me-Vision Academy Diplomas.

13. When they need to share their POE - with all their activities, learnings and practical implementations in it - we review it and extend a certificate, endorsing their Best-Self Confidence journey, as an independent party.

14. All managers can be given real-time anonymous, quantitative, and qualitative feedback by anyone who works with and for them, on the universal leadership levels of leading self, others, organisation and society. Participants simply click on their picture and follow the process. Leaders receive ratings and can customize their development around real-time feedback, ensuring they develop relevant areas, today.

15. For those who progress into managerial levels, there is Executive Club, with focused powerful personal and leadership development programs and certificates that will help them lead from their Best-Self. They will learn how to lead their self, others, organisation and society more consciously. Inside Executive Club CBS Points accumulate automatically as they complete modules and programs and their certificates are sent real-time upon completion.

Opening up a Me-Vision Academy inside your organisation ensures you give your people the tools to become Best-Self Confident. If you don’t do this the divide between expectation of Best-Self Confidence and becoming of it widens over time. That leads to dissatisfaction, failure by individuals and ultimately the organisation. Me-Vision Academy becomes a personal, career and leadership pocket and desktop coach, helping employees achieve and remain Best-Self Confident.
