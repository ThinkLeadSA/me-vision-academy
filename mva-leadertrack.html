    • Why Track Leaders?

    No, it's not about clocking their office hours. It's about an ongoing, real-time understanding of their leadership prowess. However, LeaderTrack doesn't just aim to observe and understand, it aims to elevate. It's about tracking real-time how well your leaders steer their teams and responsibilities, all in synchronization with your handpicked leadership competencies and core values, then doing something about it...real-time.  Isn’t that what happens with any top professional athlete out there? Aren’t they monitored, tracked all the time? Why should it be any different for Professional Leaders who determine our future and happiness?

    How Does LeaderTrack Work?

    No cameras on foreheads, no invasive methods. Instead, we leverage the current cutting-edge technology. Much like tracking news stories in real time through eyewitnesses and smartphones, LeaderTrack taps into your leaders' greatest resource: their colleagues. Through laptops or mobile phones, every colleague can provide anonymous, real-time feedback. The result? A constant flow of insights, analysis and individualised development  that happen instantaneously. Leaders, organisations, coaches and leadership practitioners will actually know how Leaders are doing on the playing field of leadership, at any given moment.

    What Does the LeaderTrack System “track” authentically?

    1. Potential: It tracks the true potential according to those that matter.
    2. Behavioural Change: It tracks the shifts in their leadership behaviors.
    3. Performance: It tracks their performance in a genuine light.
    4. Personal Improvement: It tracks their genuine evolution.
    5. Accountability: It tracks whether they own their development based on feedback
    6. Perceptions: It tracks the genuine perceptions above, sideways, below
    7. Development: It tracks their actual learnings on their development path
    8. Attitude: It tracks their attitude towards challenges, leadership, life.
    9. Employee Engagement: It tracks their connection with their team.
    10. Transparency: It tracks how they cultivate an authentic culture of openness.
    11. Cultural Alignment: It tracks how well they align with your organizational culture.
    12. Living Leadership Competencies: It tracks how authentically they embody your chosen competencies/values.

    LeaderTrack - Tracking your Leaders, in all divisions, on all levels and in all countries…real-time.

    The Evolution of Leadership Development

    LeaderTrack doesn't just revolutionize leadership development; it authenticates it. And LeaderTrack replaces old school leadership development with authentic continuous improvement, which becomes a way of life.

    It actually addresses an organization's unique pain points - Inefficient Leadership Development Programs; Lack of Accountability; Low Employee Engagement; Succession Planning Challenges; Inconsistent Leadership Quality; Resistance to Change; Lack of Data-Driven D ecisions; Talent Drain; Organisational Culture Enhancement; Adaptation to Remote Work; Increased Market Competition; Escalating costs.

    LeaderTrack aligns seamlessly with your leadership brand, and enriches your organizational culture...real-time. It's a genuine transformation, for genuine leaders, on the journey to genuine success.

    The benefits for organisations when implementing LeaderTrack:

    1. Behavioural Insight: LeaderTrack provides real-time insights into a leader's behaviors, enabling organizations to understand how leaders interact with their teams, make decisions, and handle various situations.

    2. Performance Enhancement: By tracking a leader's daily actions, organizations can identify areas of improvement and provide targeted support for skill development and performance enhancement.

    3. 360-Degree Perspective: LeaderTrack gathers feedback from colleagues, offering a 360-degree perspective on a leader's performance. This comprehensive view helps in identifying blind spots and areas for growth.

    4. Timely Intervention: Real-time feedback allows organizations to address issues promptly, preventing potential conflicts from escalating and ensuring a healthy work environment.

    5. Leadership Development: LeaderTrack becomes a powerful tool for leadership development. It helps leaders align their actions with organizational values, fostering a culture of continuous improvement.

    6. Customised Growth Plans: With data from LeaderTrack, organizations can create tailored development plans for leaders, focusing on their specific needs and areas of improvement.

    7. Employee Engagement: Engaging employees in the feedback process empowers them and fosters a sense of ownership in the organization's success. They feel heard and valued.

    8. Transparent Leadership: LeaderTrack promotes transparency and accountability. Leaders can demonstrate their commitment to growth by actively seeking feedback and acting on it.

    9. Cultural Alignment: The tool helps leaders align their behaviors with the organization's culture and values, reinforcing a positive work environment.

    10. Succession Planning: Tracking leadership behaviors and improvement over time aids in identifying potential future leaders and grooming them for higher responsibilities.

    11. Innovation and Adaptation: Real-time feedback encourages leaders to adapt quickly to changing circumstances, making them more agile and responsive.

    12. Organisational Effectiveness: Ultimately, LeaderTrack contributes to overall organizational effectiveness by ensuring leaders are aligned with the organization's goals, leading to improved performance and outcomes.

    In essence, LeaderTrack is more than just a tracking tool; it's a catalyst for leadership growth, development, and organizational transformation. It empowers leaders to reach their potential, aligns them with organizational values, and creates a culture of excellence and continuous  improvement.
